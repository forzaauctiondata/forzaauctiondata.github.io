Welcome to Forza Auction Data

Get the inside scoop on car auction prices in Forza Horizon 5!

Looking to get the most out of your Forza Horizon 5 Experience? Check out our helpful interactive plots of player auction data.

With this data, you can make informed decisions that can help you maximize your winnings in the Auction House.

These graphs are updated regularly, so be sure to check back often!

Finished Auctions Scatter Plots

Are you trying to find the price history for different cars that are sold at the Auction House? These dynamic price trends can help you figure out the best time and price to buy or sell your favorite cars.

The following scatter plots present data about the winning bid amount over time. 

Each item represents a successful car auction and shows information about the winning bid amount, the time it was saved, and its performance rating.
